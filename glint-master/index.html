Together for a Sustainable Future

Exploring the 17 Global Goals for Sustainable Development

In 2015, the United Nations adopted the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure peace and prosperity by 2030. These goals were built on the Millennium Development Goals and are the result of years of international collaboration, led by the UN. Today, countries across the world are working together to tackle global challenges through the SDGs, from eradicating hunger to combating climate change
